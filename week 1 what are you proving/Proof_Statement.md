# Week 1: What Are You Proving?

## 🎯 The Proof Statement

I prove to **Senior Engineering Managers hiring for backend roles at fast-paced SaaS startups** [THE PERSON] that **I can build robust, fault-tolerant data pipelines and background job queues** [THE CLAIM]. The single most important action I want them to take after seeing my portfolio is to **click the contact button and email me directly to schedule a technical screening** [THE ACTION].

---

## 🧠 The Honest "Why"

A CV or LinkedIn profile can claim *"I know Node.js and SQL"*, but this portfolio actually proves I understand and can build complex backend edge cases—like idempotency, automatic retries, data extraction, and artifact handling—that happen in real-world production environments.
