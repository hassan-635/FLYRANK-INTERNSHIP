# Week 3: Decide Once (Identity Kit)

---

## 🅰️ 1. Typography (The Type)
To keep the design cohesive and minimize decision fatigue, I am using a single, highly-readable geometric sans-serif font family across the entire site.

- **Heading Font:** `Inter` (Bold / 700)
- **Body Font:** `Inter` (Regular / 400)

---

## 🎨 2. The Palette
A tight, 3-color palette designed to be calm and professional. The UI should fade into the background so the case studies (the proof) are the loudest thing on the page.

- **Background (Near-White):** `#FAFAFA`
- **Text (Near-Black):** `#171717`
- **Main Accent (Tech Blue):** `#2563EB` *(Used sparingly for primary buttons and active links only)*

---

## 🖼️ 3. Logo / Favicon
A clean, brutally simple monogram that requires no graphic design talent but looks highly intentional.

**The Monogram:** 
A white letter **"H"** (set in Inter ExtraBold) centered inside a perfect square with a solid background of `#2563EB` (the main accent color). 

---

## 📝 4. Style Note (Added to Claude Project)
I have added the following two lines to my Claude Project instructions so AI generated CSS stays perfectly consistent:

> **Style Note:** 
> Fonts: `Inter` for everything. Colors: `#FAFAFA` (Background), `#171717` (Text), `#2563EB` (Accent). 
> Mood: Clinical, professional, and understated—the technical work must be the loudest thing on the page; do not use gradients, shadows, or excessive animations.
